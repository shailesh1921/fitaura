Core Features

Everything a smart athlete needs.

Every feature is purpose-built. No bloat, no black boxes — just intelligent, transparent fitness tooling.

🧠
Adaptive Training Engine
Adjusts load, volume, and training split based on your RPE, recovery score, sleep quality, and injury flags. Every decision comes with a plain-English explanation — no black-box AI.
Core Engine
🔥
Real-Time Muscle Heatmap
SVG body map tracking 13 muscle groups with a 7-day synergist decay engine. Click any muscle to see volume, RPE, recovery score, weekly history, and exercise recommendations.
Visual Analytics
🍽️
Nutrition Intelligence
Macro cycling engine with low-carb, high-carb, and refeed states tied to your training phase. Includes a food camera scanner, barcode lookup, and daily caloric targeting.
Diet Engine
📱
PWA — Offline Ready
Built as a Progressive Web App with a service worker. Install it to your home screen, log workouts offline, and sync when reconnected — no app store needed.
PWA
💊
Recovery & Readiness Score
CNS fatigue model that calculates your daily readiness from workout history, RPE trends, and elapsed recovery time. Automatically triggers deload recommendations when needed.
Recovery AI
📊
Performance Twin Analytics
Live charts for weight trend, calorie intake, training streak, and cardio zones. Includes an AI readout that synthesizes your data into a daily performance summary.
Analytics
🔗
Wearable Device Sync
Connect Apple Watch, Garmin, Fitbit, Whoop, Oura, Polar, Samsung, and Xiaomi bands. Real-time Bluetooth LE sync with automatic data import into your training engine.
Device Sync
🍎
Apple Health & Google Fit
Native health API integration for iOS and Android. Pull heart rate, sleep stages, steps, HRV, SpO2, and calories directly from your phone's health platform.
Health APIs
